Earpain

    1]Roast some dry turmeric in mustard oil, strain it and use it as ear drops. Apply a few drops of this oil into your ear and leave it overnight. Pus formation may stop in a fortnight causing improved hearing.

    2]Extracting juice of mango leaves or neem leaves and using it as ear drops helps too. These drops are known to have anti-inflammatory and anti-bacterial effects.

    3]Jatamansi and ashwagandha, the two most common ayurvedic herbs can be prepared together in the oil. You can use this daily in case of vertigo and tinnitus.

    4]Five cloves of garlic crushed and boiled in coconut oil and applied as ear drops are another good option for treating ear infection and pain.

    5]Few drops of Bhangra juice mixed with black tulsi poured in the affected ear can provide quick relief.

    6]Ayurveda also recommends avoiding cold and wind conditions while suffering from ear pain. It also recommends avoiding certain foods that aggravate Kapha like curd, banana, sour fruits.
